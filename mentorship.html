Our Mentorship Services

We offer personalized 1-on-1 mentorship tailored to your specific needs, whether you are a PhD student or an early-career professional in AI/ML.

For PhD Students

Get support with research strategy, paper writing and publication, experimental design, navigating advisor relationships, preparing for exams or presentations, and interview preparation (including coding interviews for Machine Learning & Computer Vision Research Engineer or Research Scientist roles).

For Early-Career Professionals

Receive guidance on technical skill enhancement (e.g., Python, ML frameworks, MLOps, cloud platforms), transitioning to industry, career planning, interview preparation (focusing on coding interviews for Machine Learning & Computer Vision Research Engineer or Research Scientist roles), and developing effective communication skills.

Potential Focus Areas Include

  • Research Strategy & Paper Writing
  • Technical Skill Development (Python, PyTorch, JAX, Cloud)
  • Algorithm Development & Implementation
  • Data Analysis & Interpretation
  • Communication & Presentation Skills
  • Career Planning & Job Search Strategy
  • Academia to Industry Transitions
  • Building a Professional Portfolio
  • Interview Preparation (Coding Interviews & Research Engineer/Scientist Roles)

Our approach is personalized: We work with you to understand your unique goals and challenges, creating a tailored mentorship plan.
